Savings programs
Michigan Golf Passbook
Statewide discount book — up to 75% off at 129 courses, 3 visits per course. ~$100 for the entire state. Pays for itself in 2-3 rounds.
Save: $100 upfront → $300-500+ in savings across a season. The single best golf value hack in Michigan.
General timing intel
Shoulder season (May, Sep-Oct) saves 20-30% vs summer peak. Twilight rates (after 3-4pm) save 30-50% at most courses. Weekday play is almost always cheaper than weekends. Book tee times 4-6 weeks ahead for peak season (Jun-Aug).
